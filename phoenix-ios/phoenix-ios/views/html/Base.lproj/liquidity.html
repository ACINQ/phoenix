Phoenix allows you to receive payments on bitcoin's blockchain layer (L1) and bitcoin's lightning layer (L2).

  • The blockchain layer (L1) is slower, and generally much more expensive (requires miner fees)
  • The lightning layer (L2) is much faster, and generally much cheaper (especially for smaller payments)

When you receive a payment on L1, Phoenix will automatically move the funds to L2 IF the miner fees adhere to your configured fee policy.

Payments you receive on L2 can be received instantly and for zero fees. However, occasionally an L1 operation is also required in order to manage the L2 payment channel. This can be done automatically IF the miner fees adhere to your configured fee policy.
